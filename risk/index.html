FORNAX EXECUTION CONSULTING
GCC Business Risk & Decision Readiness Survey
Fornax Execution Consulting is conducting this survey to understand how businesses, investors, advisors, and decision-makers across the GCC are handling business risk during the current period of regional uncertainty and changing market conditions. It asks about risk exposure, decision-making, operational readiness, governance controls, and how organizations are reviewing risk before major decisions are made or executed. The survey takes approximately 2 to 3 minutes to complete. Responses are confidential.
